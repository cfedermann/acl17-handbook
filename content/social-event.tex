\noindent The ACL 2017 Social and Networking Event will be held at the
Vancouver Aquarium located in Stanley Park, starting at 18:00. Here you
will enjoy desserts, coffee and tea, and a cash bar. Bring your kids and
enjoy a marine sanctuary in the heart of Stanley Park, home to thousands
of incredible ocean species and amazing aquatic life. Since opening in
1956, the Vancouver Aquarium has connected more than 40 million people
from around the world to our oceans and all the wonders within them.
Enjoy networking with colleagues and have a relaxing evening!
